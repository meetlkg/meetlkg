Check-in Hits

Download APK

A social network app for Students of Hindustan University.
It facilitates some unique features like
1. Students can message according to class or department wise.
2. Teachers can send notification as well as send notes and assignments
3. Student can check-in anywhere in college and let their friends know where they are.
4. Can make the phone silent during class hours.
5. Can inform, if any problem to the anonymous channel to respective authority.
