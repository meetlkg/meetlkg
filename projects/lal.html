Lock and Learn

Download APK

An Intuitive android lock screen application.

A person on average lock and unlocks their phone 50 times a day.
This gives the opportunity to use this space and time to do something really meaningful.

lock and learn was created to pace the learning of vocabulary for people giving GRE, Toefl and other exams.

When you unlock the phone, you get to learn new question/words, so this results in learning new things without applying much focus and time.

The screen allows us to drag the icon to the right answer and won't unlock till you get to the answer.
Also if you want to skip, you can swipe up twice to unlock directly.
