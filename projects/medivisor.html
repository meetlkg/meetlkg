The project is about educating and reducing the use of banned drugs.

Day by day the use of the banned drug in India is increasing due to lack of awareness in drug materials.

There are system and many functions to check the expiry or is the medicine right to the particular patients, but there is none to check if the drug is banned and if it is having side effects.

So the project mainly gives a first-hand information about the drug people are going to use and also check if it’s fit for consumption.

Statistics say that around 3.2% of all drugs given to people for dosage experience ADRs. This percentage is a big number as it deals with people‘s life.

The project is mainly based on improving the functionality of the bar code medication Administration(BCMA).
