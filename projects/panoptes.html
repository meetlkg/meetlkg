Panoptes (An assist for people with blindness)



Panoptes, An assist glass for blind people which is kind of normal glass that people wear today.
But one day this can help people with this disability to move freely like normal people.

It basically a glass consisting of a couple of cameras, with a microprocessor and a earphone.

The basic functionality it could provide would be of giving the audio output to people about the obstacles in front of them, with the character as well as object recognition and also a navigator support.

In a scenario of a blind person going for having coffee to a nearby restaurant, this glass will guide them to the place with directions and obstacle avoidance, find them an empty spot in the restaurant, would read the menu for them and might also pay for the bill.

The application of this glass is limitless.
