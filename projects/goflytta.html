Flytta

https://goflytta.com

At Flytta, our vision is to provide a hassle-free relocation service with full-scale technological automation.

Flytta is an end to end relocation platform for someone moving from one place to another.

Our technology spans from packing and moving solution to getting an aadhaar card name changed. We have 6 online portals, 4 android applications, 2 deep learning solutions, 2 machine learning based prediction models and serverless backend architecture.

I am a part of this journey, redefining how 40 million people relocate yearly, saving their time and money along with the variety of options to choose and transparency in the process of they changing their livelihood.
